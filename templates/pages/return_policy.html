Return & Refund Policy

Overview

CertiBuy is committed to customer satisfaction. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return and refund process within 30 days of delivery.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All purchases made on CertiBuy are covered by our 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:

  • You have 30 days from the delivery date to initiate a return
  • Items must be in the same condition as received (unworn, unused where applicable)
  • Return shipping is free for defective items; other returns may require prepaid labels
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of return receipt

Reasons for Return

Legitimate Return Reasons:
  • Item doesn't match the certification or description
  • Item arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong item was shipped
  • Significant discrepancy in item condition from photos/description
  • Item failed to meet promised specifications

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact Support: Email support@certibuy.com or use our return portal within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, issue description, and photos if applicable
  3. Receive Approval: Our team reviews your request (usually within 24 hours)
  4. Ship Back: Once approved, ship the item using the provided prepaid label
  5. Get Refund: After we receive and inspect the return, your refund is processed

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Processing times:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • PayPal: 2-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5-10 business days
  • Digital Wallet: Varies by provider

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned (unless defective):

  • Items returned outside the 30-day window
  • Items showing signs of use beyond normal inspection
  • Custom or personalized items (unless defective)
  • Items damaged due to negligence during return shipping
  • Clearance or final sale items (clearly marked)

Exchanges

Don't want a refund? Request an exchange instead! We can:

  • Ship a replacement or different item
  • Process priority exchanges within 24 hours
  • Upgrade or downgrade items with price adjustments

Certification & Inspection Disputes

If you believe an item's certification is inaccurate or incomplete, we take this seriously:

  • Contact us within 14 days of discovery
  • Provide details and evidence of the discrepancy
  • A senior inspector will review your case
  • Full refund or reinspection at no cost
  • Seller may be penalized or removed if negligence is found

Buyer Protection Guarantee

CertiBuy Buyer Protection covers:

  • Items that don't match the certification
  • Items significantly different from description/photos
  • Non-delivery or lost shipments
  • Unauthorized transactions

How to file a claim: Contact support within 30 days with evidence. We'll investigate and issue a full refund if the claim is valid.
